Megan Buttry

I am a college student at Milligan University pursuing a degree in Computer Science.

I grew up here in East Tennessee in my hometown of Kingsport. I started school at Kingsley Elementary, but eventually left for Tri-Cities Christian Schools, where I stayed from 2nd grade all the way until High School Graduation. I am a non-denomination Christian who grew up in the church. I live with both parents and a sister who attends ETSU.

College was a struggle for me in the beginning, but once I decided on a degree I would actually enjoy, things turned for the better. I am currently nearing the end of my degree and hoping to pursue a steady job to stick with for a long time.

I enjoy animating in my spare time, and I even created an animation ad for a local business. Animation goes hand in hand with art as a whole as I enjoy painting and drawing between assignments and work. Cooking is another hobby I enjoy as I regularly cook delicious food for family and friends.

My hopes for the future are to settle down with a job and start a family. I am already looking for a job in my degree now, even though most people probably will not hire until I am graduated.
